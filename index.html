Research

Low‑Rank Matrix Approximation for Neural Network Compression

Introduces Adaptive‑Rank SVD (ARSVD), selecting layer ranks via spectral entropy to preserve information content. Outperforms fixed‑rank SVD on ResNet18, VGG16, InceptionV3 with stronger compression–accuracy trade‑offs.

ICMLT 2025 (presented) → IEEE journal (in process)SVDSpectral EntropyPyTorchModel Compression

Fundamental Limits of Language Model Distillation under Stochastic Output Perturbations

Information‑theoretic lower bounds for black‑box distillation with randomized outputs. A Fano‑style bound ties per‑query mutual information to irreducible error; under uniform mixing with noise p, query complexity scales as (1−p)−2.

NeurIPS 2025 — Lock‑LLM Workshop (accepted)Mutual InformationFano BoundModel Extraction

The Minimax Complexity of Preference‑Based Decision Making in Multi‑Objective RL

Decision‑theoretic framework that minimizes utility regret from pairwise preferences—no reward recovery. Proves tight lower bounds governed by Pareto‑front geometry and matches them with a query‑efficient algorithm.

NeurIPS 2025 — ARLET Workshop (accepted)MinimaxRegret BoundsPareto Front

Neural Fields Meet Attention

Proves affine equivariance for Transformer operators with relative encodings + normalization, and an attention–gradient identity: linear attention computes exact negative gradients for sinusoidal fields; softmax attention converges at O(τ−2).

NeurIPS 2025 — NeurReps Proceedings (accepted)EquivarianceLinear AttentionGeometry

Phase‑Transitional Scaling

Models emergent abilities as phase transitions with a sigmoid response. Threshold TK tracks data complexity; sharpness γK reflects training dynamics. Achieves universal curve collapse and 4× better out‑of‑sample prediction than power‑law baselines.
