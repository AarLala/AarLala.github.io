Low-Rank Matrix Approximation for Neural Network Compression

Introduces Adaptive-Rank SVD (ARSVD), selecting layer ranks via spectral entropy to preserve information content. Outperforms fixed-rank SVD on ResNet18, VGG16, InceptionV3 with stronger compression–accuracy trade-offs.

ICMLT 2025 → IEEE Journal SVD Spectral Entropy Model Compression

The Minimax Complexity of Preference-Based Decision Making in Multi-Objective RL

Decision-theoretic framework that minimizes utility regret from pairwise preferences, without reward recovery. Proves tight lower bounds governed by Pareto-front geometry and matches them with a query-efficient algorithm.

NeurIPS 2025 — ARLET Workshop Minimax Regret Bounds Pareto Front

Neural Fields Meet Attention

Proves affine equivariance for Transformer operators with relative encodings + normalization, and an attention–gradient identity: linear attention computes exact negative gradients for sinusoidal fields; softmax attention converges at O(τ⁻²).

NeurIPS 2025 — NeurReps Equivariance Linear Attention Geometry

Phase-Transitional Scaling

Models emergent abilities as phase transitions with a sigmoid response. Threshold T_K tracks data complexity; sharpness γ_K reflects training dynamics. Achieves universal curve collapse and 4× better out-of-sample prediction than power-law baselines.

NeurIPS 2025 — Evolving LLM Scaling Laws Statistical Physics Emergent Abilities
